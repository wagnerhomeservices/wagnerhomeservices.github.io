FAQ

Before requesting service

What areas do you serve?

Most services are available within approximately a 1.5-hour drive of Lone Tree, Colorado. Larger hauling projects may be accepted outside the normal area depending on the job.

Are estimates free?

Yes. Submit the service request form with project details and photos when possible. Pricing is confirmed before work begins.

What payment methods do you accept?

Invoices are sent through Square. Payment terms and any required deposit are confirmed with your estimate.

Do you work weekends?

Weekend appointments are available by request and include a 25% surcharge.

What work do you not perform?

Wagner Home Services does not perform work requiring permits or licensed electrical, plumbing or HVAC trades.
