FAQ

Before you order

Are the storage-rack photos real?

The product images are AI-assisted mockups that illustrate the standard sizes. Actual racks are handcrafted, so lumber color, grain and small construction details may vary.

When is payment due?

A 50% deposit is due after the order is reviewed and invoiced through Square. The remaining balance is due at pickup or delivery.

How does Colorado delivery work?

Delivery is $0.72 per mile with a $20 minimum. Deliveries beyond roughly 1.5 hours from Lone Tree are grouped into scheduled routes, normally on the last Thursday of each month.

Are totes included?

No. Black or clear totes with red or yellow lids may be added for $9.99 each in any combination.

Can I order a custom width?

Yes, by request. Additional casters may be required, and the standard 65-lb-per-position rating cannot be guaranteed on custom spans.
